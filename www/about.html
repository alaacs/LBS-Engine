GeoFreebie

Welcome to GeoFreebie, a location-based mobile application that helps you find or donate gifts easily by visualising them on a map. Tap the map button at the bottom left side of the screen to find gifts on the map. Tap the list button on the bottom left side of the screen for a list view of items available. Furthermore, GeoFreebie notifies you about gifts in your current vicinity. You control if you want to make your current location public. Have fun hunting freebies!
